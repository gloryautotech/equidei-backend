1. Definition and Interpretation

1.1.  Definition: In this Addendum, unless the context otherwise requires, all capitalised words and expressions used but not defined herein shall have the same meaning attributed to them as under the provisions of the Trust Deed. In case of any conflict between the terms hereof and the terms of the Trust Deed, the terms hereof shall prevail.

1.2.  Interpretation: Clause 1.2 of the Trust Deed shall apply as if specifically incorporated in this Addendum and all references to “this Deed” in such clause shall be deemed to be a reference to this Addendum.

2. Scope of the Addendum

2.1.  This Addendum shall be read with the Trust Deed and shall be deemed to be an integral and binding part of the Trust Deed. The Parties hereby agree that the terms of this Addendum read with the Trust Deed (to the extent modified, if any) shall govern the terms of understanding of the Parties and the Parties expressly agree that the same shall be effective from {date}

2.2.   The Parties agree that in reference to the Trust Property, each of the Settlors herein has brought in the additional corpus (“Additional Corpus”) as provided in Annexure A, which shall be deemed to be part of the Trust Property.

2.3.   The Parties agree that the beneficiaries to the Trust and their Beneficial Interest in the Trust Property shall be as more fully described under Annexure B.

2.4.   The Parties hereby agree that all other terms of the Trust Deed, which are not amended expressly under this Addendum, shall remain in full effect and force. This Addendum together with the Trust Deed shall provide the entire understanding between the Parties on the subject matter. In case of any conflict between the provisions of the Trust Deed and this Addendum, the Trustee shall execute an amendment to the Trust Deed to ensure that such inconsistency is removed.

2.5. 3.1.  The Parties hereby agree and acknowledge that nothing contained in this Addendum shall affect / jeopardize or be deemed or construed as a waiver of any provision of the Trust Deed.

3.3.  Each Party hereby agree and covenant to obtain all resolutions, consents and approvals as required to be obtained by them to enable them lawfully to enter into, exercise their rights and comply with their obligations in this Addendum.

4. The Parties hereby agree and acknowledge that this Addendum shall bear reference to the clauses of the Trust Deed, and all provisions of this Addendum shall be read together with the Trust Deed. Except to the extent specifically modified hereunder, all other clauses of the Trust Deed shall be valid and enforceable to the extent permitted under the Applicable Laws.

5.  Under the Trust Deed, all references to the term “this Deed” shall be read to include the terms modified/supplemented to the Trust Deed by way of this Addendum.
